# Clinical Note — Post-ICU Follow-Up Visit

## Subjective

**Chief Concern:** Post-hospitalization follow-up and general check-up.

**History of Present Illness:**
Mr. Larson is a patient with a recent complicated hospitalization involving sepsis, septic shock, ventilator-dependent respiratory failure with ARDS, and hospital-associated anemia. He presents for a routine follow-up and health/social needs assessment.

Since discharge he reports gradual improvement. He walks daily ("once around the block") but notes reduced exercise tolerance — dyspnea on exertion with hills and stairs. **Pain:** Chronic low back discomfort rated 1/10, positional (worse with prolonged sitting), described as "background noise."
